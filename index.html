Analysis Of Initial Performance Assessment.

Immediately following the collection of your golf swing data, your golf instructor will enter the analysis stage of our process and begin to build your personalized practice program specifically targeting your goals. The analysis process is very straight forward. It starts with understanding what the ball is doing, followed by understanding what the club is doing to allow the ball to perform in that manner, and concluded with understanding what the body is doing to allow the club to perform in that fashion (K-Vest.) At this time, your golf instructor will validate the results of your physical screen and confirm why some of your swing compensations may have been born. This allows our team to start taking corrective action by truly targeting the true cause of any un-wanted swing compensations, which is imperative in preventing them from showing up ever again!

Smart Program

24 total sessions (12 Fitness & 12 Golf), to be used within a 3-month period. Up to four (4) can be flexed for max of 16 and min of 8 each discipline. Includes unlimited use of private trackman simulator practice.

Smarter Program

48 total sessions (24 Fitness & 24 Golf), to be used within a 3-month period. Up to six (6) can be flexed for max of 30 and min of 18 in each discipline. Includes unlimited use of private trackman simulator practice.

Smart Loop

18 total sessions (9 Fitness & 9 Golf), to be used within a 3-month period. Up to three (3) can be flexed for max of 12 and min of 6 in each discipline. Includes 4 hours of private simulator practice each month.

Smart Sampler

12 total sessions (6 Fitness & 6 Golf), to be used within a 3-month period. Up to two (2) can be flexed for max of 8 and min of 4 in each discipline. Includes 2 hours of private simulator practice each month.

Smart Dozen

6 total sessions (3 Fitness & 3 Golf), to be used within a 1-month period. Up to one (1) session can be flexed for a max of 4 and min of 3 in each discipline. Includes 2 hours of private simulator practice during the month.
